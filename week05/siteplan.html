Finance Management Portal - Site Plan

Author: Tiago Borges

Course: WDD 131 - Dynamic Web Fundamentals | BYU-Idaho

Site Name

Name: SmartFinance Portal

This name reflects a modern, informative platform focused on empowering users with the knowledge and tools they need to manage personal finances effectively!

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to provide accessible and actionable financial education to help users budget wisely, save consistently, invest strategically and plan effectively for their financial goals

Scenarios

  • How can I create a monthly budget that works for my lifestyle?
  • What are the best beginner strategies for investing?
  • How do I set and achieve my long-term financial goals?

Color Schema

  • #003366 - Used for headers, navigation bar, and accents
  • #f4f4f4 - Background color for overall layout
  • #ffffff - Section and card backgrounds
  • #4CAF50 - Call-to-action buttons and highlights

Typography

  • Open Sans - Primary font for body text
  • Roboto Slab - Used for headings and titles
